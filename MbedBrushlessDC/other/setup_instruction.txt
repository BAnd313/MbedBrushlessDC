**** Necessary material ***********
_ Nucleo board (F302R8 or F446RE with respective programs)
_ Expansion board X-NUCLEO-IHM07M1
_ Gimbal Motor 5208 (or Gimbal Motor 5206)
_ AS5601 Encoder
***********************************


** Nucleo settings ****************
_ JP1 open
_ JP5 on E5V
_ JP6 close
***********************************


** Expansion Board settings *******

Cable
_ Connect the 3 cables of the motor in J2
_ Connect power cables in J1 (8V to 48V DC)
_ 3CN10 to SDA encoder
_ 5CN10 to SCL encoder
_ 16CN7 to +3V3 encoder
_ 20CN7 to GND encoder

Jumper
_ JP1 open
_ JP2 open
_ J5 on 1Sh
_ J6 on 1Sh
_ J9 open (for Vin > 12V)

***********************************


** Motor phases *******************

With the rotor resting on the table, connect the three phases to the expansion board in the order they are.
